## SOURCE DOCUMENT: What Pabandi Live Selling Is

Pabandi is the first trust-backed live commerce platform. It connects live sellers on TikTok, YouTube, and Shopify to buyers through one universal booking link with built-in escrow protection and crypto rewards.

The core problem: Live selling is booming globally — it's a $600 billion market in China and growing fast in the US, Pakistan, and Southeast Asia. But the ecosystem is broken. Sellers broadcast on TikTok or YouTube, then desperately paste PayPal links and Venmo handles in chat. Buyers have zero protection. Sellers have zero accountability. There's no trust layer between "I want that" and "I got scammed."

Pabandi fixes this with three innovations:

### Innovation 1: One Catalog, Every Platform
A seller publishes their product catalog once inside Pabandi. That same catalog — same prices, same inventory, same descriptions — syncs across TikTok Live, YouTube Shopping, and Shopify Live simultaneously. The seller gets one universal link: `pabandi.com/s/yourname`. They paste it in their TikTok bio, their YouTube description, their WhatsApp status. Done. One link to rule them all.

### Innovation 2: Escrow-Backed Instant Checkout
When a buyer clicks that link during a live show, they don't land on a sketchy Google Form or a random PayPal page. They land on the seller's Pabandi profile — a verified, trust-scored identity with full escrow checkout. The buyer commits with a deposit. The funds are held in escrow until the transaction is fulfilled. No chargebacks for sellers. No scams for buyers. Both sides have skin in the game.

### Innovation 3: $PAB Crypto Rewards for Reliability
Here's where it gets interesting. Buyers who show up reliably — who honor their bookings, who don't ghost — earn $PAB tokens. These are real Solana-based crypto rewards that accumulate in their Pabandi wallet. The more reliable you are as a buyer, the more you earn. This creates a loyalty flywheel: reliable buyers get priority access to live shows and better deals. Sellers want reliable buyers. Buyers want rewards. Everyone wins.

## How It Works — Step by Step

Step 1: The seller registers on Pabandi and connects their platforms. This takes about 30 seconds per platform. They click "Connect TikTok Live," authorize via OAuth, and they're linked. Same for YouTube Shopping and Shopify Live. All three can be connected simultaneously.

Step 2: The seller publishes their catalog. They add items with titles, descriptions, and prices. This catalog lives on Pabandi and is accessible from any platform the seller streams on. They can also schedule upcoming shows — setting dates, times, and which platform each show will be on.

Step 3: The seller goes live. They start their TikTok Live or YouTube stream as normal. In their bio or chat, they share their Pabandi seller link. During the stream, the seller can also log orders in real-time from the Pabandi seller dashboard, tracking buyer names, items, and amounts as they come in.

Step 4: Buyers join from anywhere. A viewer sees the link, clicks it, and lands on the seller's Pabandi profile. They see the catalog, pick what they want, and book instantly with escrow-backed checkout. No app download required. No account creation wall. Just open, book, done.

Step 5: Escrow protects both sides. The buyer's deposit is held securely. When the seller fulfills the order — ships the item, delivers the service — the escrow releases. If something goes wrong, the buyer is protected. If the buyer ghosts, the seller keeps the commitment deposit.

Step 6: Both sides build trust. Every completed transaction builds the seller's Pabandi Passport trust score. Every reliable booking builds the buyer's reliability score and earns them $PAB rewards. Over time, both sides accumulate portable, verifiable reputation that follows them across every platform.

## The Pabandi Passport — Why It Matters

Every seller on Pabandi has a Passport profile. This is a portable identity that includes their trust score, transaction history, platform connections, and buyer reviews. Think of it like a credit score for commerce reliability. A seller with a high trust score gets more buyer confidence. A buyer with a high reliability score gets priority access and better deals.

This is the layer that doesn't exist anywhere else. TikTok doesn't have it. YouTube doesn't have it. Shopify doesn't have it. Pabandi is the trust infrastructure that sits between all of them.

## The Technology Stack

Pabandi is built on React and TypeScript for the frontend, Node.js and Express for the backend, PostgreSQL for the database, and Solana blockchain for the $PAB token and escrow logic. It integrates with Meta Cloud API for WhatsApp notifications — so sellers get WhatsApp alerts when buyers book from their live shows. Zero extra setup required.

The platform supports three live commerce integrations today: TikTok Live, YouTube Shopping, and Shopify Live. Each integration connects via OAuth and syncs with the seller's universal Pabandi catalog.
