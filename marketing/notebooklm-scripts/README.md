## How to Use with NotebookLM

1. Go to [notebooklm.google.com](https://notebooklm.google.com)
2. Create a new notebook
3. Upload one of the script files (01, 02, or 03) as a source
4. Click **Audio Overview** to generate the narrated audio
5. Download or share the generated audio/video

### Tips for best results:
- Upload **one script per notebook** for focused output
- The scripts are already formatted for natural narration — no editing needed
- For the short video (02), the output will be tight and punchy by design
- For the explainer (03), the sectioned structure produces natural transitions
- You can also paste the scripts into NotebookLM's chat and ask it to "generate an audio overview"
