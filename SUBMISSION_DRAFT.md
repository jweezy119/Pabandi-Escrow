### Description (few words)
Solidity escrow on Monad Testnet for booking reservations — PabandiEscrow.sol

### Problem
Booking no-shows silently drain revenue from Pakistani SMEs — salons, restaurants, hotels, clinics. Today customers book for free, ghost, and the business eats the loss. There is no financial accountability layer in the digital reservation flow.

### Solution
PabandiEscrow.sol is a Solidity smart contract on Monad Testnet that locks customer funds at booking time. Business claims the deposit after successful check-in. If the business no-shows past a 48-hour deadline, the customer automatically receives a full onchain refund. A dispute flag lets either party escalate to platform review. This replaces trust with code.
