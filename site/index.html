✦ Features

Everything your business
needs to win.

From automated deposits to webhook integrations with your existing CRM — Pabandi slots in, not over.

🤖

AI No-Show Risk Scoring

Each booking is scored in real time using customer history, weather, traffic, local events, and time-of-day patterns. Score updates as conditions change.

💳

Dynamic Deposit Capture

High-risk bookings trigger automatic deposit requests via Safepay. Supports credit/debit cards, JazzCash, and EasyPaisa. Funds settle directly to your account.

📊

Real-Time Analytics

Track booking patterns, no-show rates, revenue protected, and table turnover in a clean dashboard. Export to CSV or connect via webhook.

🔔

Automated Reminders

SMS via Twilio and email via SendGrid reminders go out automatically at 24h, 2h, and 30 minutes before every reservation.

🔗

Webhooks & CRM Integration

Push booking events to your POS, CRM, or any system via outbound webhooks. Full REST API available for custom integrations.

🌐

Global & Local Compliance

PCI-DSS & PDPB compliant data handling. Stripe & Safepay local payment gateways. Multi-lingual support built for global scale.
