# Pabandi — Global Advancement Brief
Reframed for user understanding, not internal roadmap language. Each advancement is ordered by first impression impact.

## Core Principle
Users should understand Pabandi in 8 seconds or less. Trust, automation, and frictionless experience are the proof—not the pitch.

---

## Advancement 1: Interactive Escrow Sandbox (Homepage Hero)
### The Moment
A visitor lands on pabandi.com and immediately sees a live demo of the core mechanic: the money moves only when both sides win.

### What They See
- Split-screen visual: Guest on the left, Solana Vault in the center, Host on the right.
- A draggable booking control.
- When dragged: funds visually lock into the vault.
- When host confirms check-in: vault opens, funds flow to host, $PAB reward flies back to guest.

### Why It Works
People trust what they can see. Static text about “trustless escrow” fails. This demo makes the safety mechanism obvious without requiring a finance background.

### Open Question Answer
Move the current map view below the fold. The sandbox owns the hero because it explains the product; the map proves coverage. Both matter, but the explanation must come first.

---

## Advancement 2: Qwen-VL Global KYC Scanner (Trust Profile)
### The Moment
A new user opens their profile and sees a single button: “Verify instantly.” They point their camera at a passport or government ID. Two seconds later they have a verified badge and their Trust Score jumps.

### What They See
- Profile page with a clear “Verify” prompt.
- Camera or upload flow.
- Real-time extraction status: name, document type, verification state.
- Privacy notice: “Your image is not stored. Ever.”

### Why It Works
Manual KYC drop-off is brutal. Global launch requires global ID support. Multimodal AI removes the friction and makes verification feel futuristic, not bureaucratic.

### Open Question Answer
Destroy the image immediately after extraction. PII liability is a brand killer. If compliance ever demands retention, encrypt and auto-delete within 24 hours. Privacy-first messaging is a competitive advantage.

---

## Advancement 3: AI Smart Dispute Resolution (Reservations)
### The Moment
A host marks a guest as a no-show. The guest opens the dispute panel, uploads a selfie or venue photo, and receives an impartial AI recommendation within seconds—no support ticket, no waiting.

### What They See
- Reservation detail with a clear “Dispute” path.
- Upload flow for evidence.
- AI recommendation panel showing rationale based on image context, booking time, location, and venue confirmation.
- Final fund release or refund animation.

### Why It Works
Web3 escrow needs trustless resolution. AI delivers speed and impartiality at scale. This positions Pabandi as the platform that literally holds the money and judges the truth—without humans.

---

## Recommended Build Order
1. Advancement 1 — Interactive Escrow Sandbox (hero)
2. Advancement 2 — Qwen-VL Global KYC Scanner (trust)
3. Advancement 3 — AI Smart Dispute Resolution (reservations)

### Rationale
Hero first drives comprehension. Trust second drives activation. Resolution third drives retention and viral trust stories.

---

## Success Signal
A new user can explain Pabandi to a friend after 10 seconds on the homepage. That is the metric.
