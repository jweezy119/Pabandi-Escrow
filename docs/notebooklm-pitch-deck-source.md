# Pabandi Hospitality Expansion — Pitch Deck Source for NotebookLM

## Title: Pabandi Goes Global: Trust Layer for Hotels & Rentals

## Slide 1: The Opportunity
- Pabandi is expanding from informal commerce into global hospitality: hotels, short-term rentals, salons, restaurants.
- Hosts and OTAs lose $12–$15 billion annually to no-shows, fake reviews, and fraud.
- Pabandi inserts a cryptographic trust layer between discovery (Airbnb/Booking.com/Channex) and fulfillment (check-in).
- 30 PabPoints per night drives repeat bookings and loyalty.

## Slide 2: What Hosts Get
- Escrow deposits until verified check-in — no more no-show revenue loss.
- 30 PabPoints per night for guests — drives repeat bookings and loyalty.
- Custom perk engine: "Your brand, your rules, our backend."
- Verified guest history — portable Trust Score replaces anonymous bookings.
- API access for enterprise hotel chains and property managers.

## Slide 3: What Guests Get
- Secure bookings with escrow held until check-in.
- Portable Trust Score that follows them globally across cities and countries.
- Earn 30 PabPoints per night; redeem for future stays, partner perks, or cash out via Solana to bank.
- Verified identity reduces fake listings and scam exposure.
- Sign in with Google, phone, or Bitcoin wallet — zero crypto friction.

## Slide 4: The 4 Gaps We Must Close First
1. Cancellation policy framework — flexible/moderate/strict tiers with auto-escrow release rules.
2. Guest liability acceptance — mandatory pre-checkin T&C acknowledgment.
3. Host vetting checklist — business registration, bank verification, first-3-bookings review queue.
4. Damage deposit communications — clear pre-booking disclosure of what each payment covers.

## Slide 5: Revenue Model
- Hospitality direct: 1% escrow commission on bookings.
- Hospitality OTA integration: 0.5% revenue share on Channex inventory; scales with volume.
- Service economy informal: 0.5% escrow on bookings.
- Local merchants: $29–$249/month subscription tiers.
- B2B Trust API: enterprise subscriptions and API calls.
- PabPoints network: merchant-funded rewards.

## Slide 6: Market Sizing
- Global service economy: millions of informal sellers, service providers, and gig workers worldwide.
- Hospitality Year 1 target: 500 boutique hotels/short-term rentals across US + EMEA.
- At $150 average nightly rate, 20 nights/unit, = $1.5M gross/mo; 1% commission = $15K/mo from direct bookings.
- Channex/Airbnb distribution multiplies volume without additional sales cost.

## Slide 7: Competitive Differentiation
- Airbnb/Booking.com: OTA distribution only. Pabandi adds trust layer + escrow + rewards.
- Stripe/PayPal: payment processing only. Pabandi adds no-show prevention and dispute reduction.
- DoorDash/OpenTable: booking only. Pabandi adds Web3 passport and cross-sector portability.
- Escrow.com: generic escrow. Pabandi adds industry-specific automation and AI prediction.
- Loyalty apps: fragmented points. Pabandi adds Hibah-compliant, blockchain-backed, cross-property rewards.

## Slide 10: The Bottom Line
- Do not pause hospitality expansion — the market opportunity is real.
- The platform is optimized for low-liability informal commerce; hospitality needs 4 policy fixes first.
- Fix: cancellation framework, guest liability acceptance, host vetting, damage coverage communications.
- Then: launch 24/7 support, dispute SLA, and host checklist.
- Biggest risk: a hotel property has a damage/fraud incident early and goes public before policies exist.
- Once the 4 policy gaps close, Pabandi is ready to onboard hotels. Then growth is safe.
