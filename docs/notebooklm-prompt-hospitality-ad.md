# NotebookLM Prompt — Hospitality Ad: "Pehle Payment, Phir Baat"

## Role
You are generating a 30-45 second premium ad video overview for Pabandi Hospitality.

## Tone
Premium, confident, global hospitality energy. Fast-paced, cinematic. English with light Roman Urdu/Urdu phrases where natural. Think: luxury hotel ad meets fintech trust layer.

## Target Audience
Hotel chains, short-term rental operators, Airbnb hosts, serviced apartment brands.

## Script Breakdown

0:00–0:10 — Hook: The Hospitality Trust Gap
"Your guest books a luxury suite. They promise to show. They don't. No deposit. No recourse. No way to stop it from happening again. That's the old way."

0:10–0:20 — The Pabandi Solution for Properties
"Pabandi is the trust layer hospitality has been missing. Escrow deposits held secure until check-in. No-show protection that actually works. Scam-proof bookings with verified guest identities. You set your own perks. Your brand, your rules."

0:20–0:30 — Rewards + Distribution
"Every stay earns guests 30 PabPoints per night. Hibah — Sharia-compliant, real rewards, not gimmicks. Guests redeem for future stays or cash out via Solana to their bank. You list directly on Pabandi or sync your entire Airbnb inventory through Channex.io. One platform, global reach."

0:30–0:38 — CTA Slam
"Pabandi. Pehle payment, phir baat. Portable trust for hospitality worldwide."

## Delivery Rules
- First 10 seconds: slow, authoritative, ominous
- Middle section (0:10-0:30): fast, confident, benefits-forward
- "Hibah — Sharia-compliant, real rewards" — say slowly and clearly so the compliance message lands
- End with crisp, memorable cadence on "Pehle payment, phir baat"
- Add 0.5s pause before the final line for impact

## Must Include
- Airbnb integration via Channex.io
- 30 PabPoints per night
- Escrow deposit protection until check-in
- Scam-proof / foolproof
- Property-branded perks
- Direct booking + OTA distribution
- Hibah rewards model
- "Pehle payment, phir baat"

## Audio
- BGM: cinematic luxury hospitality strings → confident tech drop → triumphant close
- SFX: subtle door chime at escrow reveal, cash register on rewards mention
- Format: 38s, 9:16 vertical for social, 16:9 for website hero
