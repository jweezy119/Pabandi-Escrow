## HEADLINE
**Pabandi Launches AI Reliability Layer for the Global Service Economy**

## SUBHEAD
The first platform turning everyday punctuality into verifiable rewards — for salons, restaurants, real estate agents, and service providers.

---

## THE PROBLEM
The global service economy runs on trust, reminders, and missed revenue.  
- Salon, restaurant, and rental bookings increasingly happen through live video, chat, and reservation tools  
- Service providers lose significant income to last-minute cancellations and no-shows  
- Businesses spend heavily on reminders that still fail  
- There is no portable, verifiable trust score that follows clients across brands

## THE SOLUTION
**Pabandi** means “punctuality” / “commitment.” The platform makes that trait measurable:

1. **AI No-Show Prediction** — Learns booking patterns to flag high-risk appointments before they happen
2. **Smart Deposits** — Booking deposits that protect businesses while treating good clients better
3. **Rewards** — Users earn points and perks for showing up; businesses earn for honoring bookings
4. **Portable Reputation** — A cross-app reliability score customers can carry across brands and platforms

## HOW IT WORKS (USER JOURNEY)
```
Search → Book → Pay deposit if needed → AI predicts risk → Show up → Earn reward
                                          → No-show → Deposit released to business
```

## TOKEN & REWARDS MODEL
- **Reward unit:** PabPoints
- **Utility:** Booking rewards, deposit benefits, status perks, API payments
- **Model:** Stable points-based rewards; optional blockchain-backed verification

## TARGET MARKETS (LAUNCH SEQUENCE)
1. **Phase 1 — US & global launch:** Salons, restaurants, short-term rentals, clinics, freelancers
2. **Phase 2 — Global expansion:** Southeast Asia, MENA, and Latin America
3. **Phase 3 — Platform integrations:** Commerce ecosystems and hospitality PMS tools

## KEY DIFFERENTIATORS
- Built for global service culture — multilingual, mobile-first, culturally adaptable
- Privacy-first: no scraping personal messages or social graph harvesting
- Customer-obsessed support and compliance-first design
- Open API: developers can build trust-enabled experiences on top of Pabandi
