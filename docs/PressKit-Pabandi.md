## HEADLINE
**Pabandi Launches AI + Web3 Reliability Layer for Pakistan’s $15B Service Economy**

## SUBHEAD
The first platform turning cultural punctuality (“pabandi”) into programmable rewards — starting with salons, clinics, and freelancers.

---

## THE PROBLEM
Pakistan’s informal service economy runs on trust, WhatsApp, and hope.  
- 60%+ of salon bookings in Karachi/Lahore are made via WhatsApp voice notes  
- Freelancers lose an estimated 20% of income to client no-shows  
- Businesses spend millions on reminders that still fail  
- There is no portable, verifiable trust score — reputation dies with each app

## THE SOLUTION
**Pabandi** (پابندی) means “regularity” or “punctuality” in Urdu. The platform makes that trait programmable:

1. **AI No-Show Prediction** — Learns booking patterns to flag high-risk appointments before they happen
2. **Trustless Deposits** — SOL/SPL escrow locks a small deposit; released to the business only on no-show
3. **$PAB Reward Token** — Users earn tokens for showing up; businesses earn for honoring bookings
4. **Portable Reputation** — A cross-app “Trust Score” that follows you across salons, clinics, and freelance platforms

## HOW IT WORKS (USER JOURNEY)
```
Book → Deposit (optional) → AI predicts risk → Show up → Earn $PAB
                                              → No-show → Deposit forfeited to business
```

## TOKEN ECONOMICS (SNAPSHOT)
- **Token:** $PAB (Solana SPL)
- **Total Supply:** 1,000,000,000 PAB
- **Utility:** Booking deposits, dynamic rewards, API payments, governance (future)
- **Status:** Mint authority revoked; supply permanently capped

## TARGET MARKETS (LAUNCH SEQUENCE)
1. **Phase 1 — Pakistan:** Salons, barbershops, clinics, diagnostics, freelancers
2. **Phase 2 — GCC Diaspora:** Overseas Pakistanis booking services for family back home
3. **Phase 3 — Global South:** Countries where trust-based commerce dominates (Nigeria, Bangladesh, Kenya)

## KEY DIFFERENTIATORS
- Built *for* Pakistan, not *in* Pakistan — founder is a US-based Pakistani who studied the market remotely for 12 months before writing a line of code
- Privacy-first: no WhatsApp message scraping, no social graph harvesting
- Shariah-conscious design: “earn-by-action” model mirrors Islamic values of *amanah* (trustworthiness)
- Open API: developers can build booking dApps on top of Pabandi’s trust layer
