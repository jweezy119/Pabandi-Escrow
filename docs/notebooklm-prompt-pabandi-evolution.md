# NotebookLM Prompt — Pabandi Evolution Skit

## Role
You are generating a 42-second energetic product evolution video overview for Pabandi.

## Tone
Fast-paced, excited, customer-obsessed. South Asian energy with Roman Urdu + English mix. Think: tech product launch at a chai dhaba. Pam is the enthusiastic founder showing off. Fiza is the skeptical customer turned advocate.

## Characters
- **Pam:** Pabandi product lead. Fast, passionate, obsessed with customer feedback. Talks like she's showing off her favorite child.
- **Fiza:** Real estate agent/salon owner. Pabandi user since v1.0. Sarcastic, slow to admit she's impressed, but when she is, it lands hard.

## Script Breakdown

0:00–0:08 — The Old Days
Fiza: "So this is Pabandi 1.0. Basic bookings. Manual deposits. I had to chase every client myself. No-shows were killing me."
Pam (bursting in): "That was then. This is now."

0:08–0:16 — Feedback Loop
Pam: "You said: 'I forgot my password.' So we added Bitcoin wallet sign-in. No more passwords. Just your wallet."
Fiza (blinking): "Wait. That's a thing now?"
Pam: "You said: 'My trust score is stuck here.' So we made it portable. Passport Trust Score, 0 to 1000, follows you worldwide. Platinum, Gold, Silver, Bronze."
Fiza: "So if I go to Dubai, my score comes with me?"
Pam: "Exactly. One score, every property."

0:16–0:26 — Feature Explosion
Pam (rapid-fire): "You said: 'I want rewards for showing up.' So we built PabPoints. Hibah — Sharia-compliant, not crypto gambling. 30 points per night. Redeem for future stays or cash out via Solana to your bank. Show up, earn, cash out. Real money."
Fiza: "Wait, like actual bank money?"
Pam: "Like actual bank money. You said: 'My hotel's on Airbnb but I can't sync.' So we integrated Channex.io. One click, every OTA. Your inventory, your rates, your brand."
Fiza: "And the scammers?"
Pam: "Escrow deposits. Funds held until check-in. Verified IDs. Zero-knowledge fraud layer. Scam-proof. Foolproof."
Fiza (slowly): "Okay. That's... actually good." [0.5s pause for comedic effect]

0:26–0:34 — Speed of Obsession
Pam (passionate): "Here's the thing. We don't build features and hope you like them. You tell us what hurts. We fix it. Usually within days. That's not a roadmap. That's a promise."
Fiza: "So if I say I want something new..."
Pam: "You'll probably have it by Friday."

0:34–0:42 — CTA Slam
Fiza: "Pabandi. Because your feedback doesn't go into a black hole. It becomes the product."
Pam: "Pehle payment, phir baat. Portable trust for hospitality worldwide."
Fiza (quiet mic-drop): "And I get actual points for showing up on time now."

## Delivery Rules
- Pam speaks fast, excited, slightly breathless — like she's showing off her favorite child
- Fiza starts slow, skeptical, gradually impressed
- When Pam says "Like actual bank money," slow down and emphasize "actual"
- When Pam says "You'll probably have it by Friday," say it with absolute, unshakeable certainty
- End with Fiza's quiet line as a silent mic-drop
- Add 0.5s pause after "That's... actually good" for comedic effect
- Pam never sounds salesy — sounds like she genuinely cares about Fiza's feedback

## Must Include
- Evolution from v1.0 to current
- Customer feedback explicitly driving features
- Speed of iteration: "72 hours," "by Friday"
- Bitcoin wallet sign-in
- Passport Trust Score tiers
- PabPoints / Hibah model
- 30 PabPoints per night
- Channex.io / Airbnb integration
- Escrow deposit protection
- Scam-proof / foolproof
- Property-branded perks
- WhatsApp automation mention
- "Pehle payment, phir baat"

## Audio
- BGM: confidence-building tech strings → triumphant crescendo at CTA
- SFX: keyboard clicks, notification pings, cash register, feature-reveal whooshes
- Format: 42s, 9:16 vertical for Reels/TikTok, 16:9 for website/Demo Day
