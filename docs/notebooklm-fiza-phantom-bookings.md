# Fiza's Phantom Bookings — Source Material

## Character
- **Auntie Fiza**: Salon owner in Karachi. Sarcastic, dramatic, exhausted. Uses Roman Urdu + English mix. Treats her salon like a kingdom.
- **Voice**: Upper-middle-class Karachi woman. Fast, expressive, dry humor. Think: "Bhabi from Defence Housing Authority who has seen it all."

## Core Pain Point
- Ghost bookings / no-shows drain cash flow
- Customers book 2 hours but only show for 15 minutes
- "SparklePrincess99" type clients who vanish after reserving prime slots
- Wasted chair time = lost money

## Pabandi Solution (simple language)
- Upfront deposit before booking is confirmed
- No deposit = no reservation
- If customer no-shows, salon keeps deposit (biryani money)
- Trust Score shows who flakes vs who pays
