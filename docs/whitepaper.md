## 5. How Pabandi Works — Simple Version

### Three Pieces, One System

**Piece 1: The Seller / Host**
- Seller/host signs up free at `pabandi.com`
- Seller creates a booking link in 2 minutes or syncs existing Airbnb/OTA inventory via **Channex.io**
- Seller sets deposit ranges, custom perks, and branding
- Seller shares the link anywhere: Instagram, TikTok, WhatsApp, website, or OTA channel
- When someone books, Pabandi automatically collects a deposit before the seller even has to reply
- Seller sees only the booking and deposit status — never the buyer's raw score

**Piece 2: The Buyer / Guest**
- Buyer clicks the link, selects a date/time, sees the deposit amount
- If buyer has a high Trust Score, deposit is zero or very small
- If buyer is new or has a lower score, deposit is fair and proportional — and the app explains exactly why
- Buyer pays via Stripe, Safepay, bank transfer, Apple/Google Pay, or crypto — all within the app
- Money is held safely in escrow until the booking is completed
- Buyer shows up → deposit released instantly → buyer earns points
- Buyer ghosts → deposit partially returned to seller as compensation
- Buyers can also sign in with Bitcoin wallet for Web3-native identity

**Piece 3: The Trust Score**
- Every user gets a score between 0 and 1000 across five tiers: **Unrated, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum**
- Score goes up when you show up
- Score goes down when you miss or cancel late
- Score is **portable** — it follows you to any Pabandi-integrated platform
- Score is **transparent** — you always see why it is what it is
- Score is **yours** — you can export it and take it with you

**Piece 4: The Rewards Engine**
- Guests earn **30 $PAB tokens per night** for every completed stay
- Hosts set custom property-branded perks tied to $PAB tokens
- $PAB tokens are Sharia-compliant **Hibah** (gifts), not interest-based staking
- Guests can redeem $PAB tokens for future bookings, partner perks, or convert via $PAB tokens → Solana → bank payment

### The Deposit Logic, Made Simple

Think of it like a driver's license:

| Your Trust Score | Deposit Required | Why |
|-----------------|------------------|-----|
| **70–100** | **0%** | You are a safe driver. We trust you completely. |
| **40–69** | **5–15%** | You are new here or booked last minute. Small hold to secure your spot. |
| **0–39** | **20–50%** | Your history shows risk. Higher deposit protects both of us. |

**Never more than 50% deposit. Never a random amount. Always explained.**

### From Zero to First Booking — Step by Step

**Seller side (2 minutes):**
1. Open `pabandi.com` → Sign up
2. Click "Create booking link"
3. Set your service name, price, and deposit range
4. Copy the link → paste in Instagram bio/TikTok bio/WhatsApp About
5. Done. Every booking through that link is protected.

**Buyer side (30 seconds):**

2. Pick a date/time
3. See deposit amount + reason

## 7. Two-Way Transparency: Un-Gameable Business Scoring

True trust cannot be a one-way street. If Pabandi requires consumers to hold themselves to a gold standard of reliability, businesses must be held to that exact same benchmark.

Traditional online review systems are fundamentally broken — polluted by paid bots, fake negative reviews from competitors, or emotional spam from people who have never even crossed the threshold of the storefront. Pabandi fixes this entirely by engineering a **Dynamic Business Scoring System** rooted in absolute cryptographic proof.

On our platform, customers can research business reliability based on reviews that cannot be faked. Every single score, rating, and piece of feedback is tied directly to:
- **Verified on-chain activity**
- **Actual booking records**
- **Geolocation check-ins** via GPS + Meta Cloud and Google Maps integrations

If someone leaves a review on Pabandi, the ecosystem verifies in milliseconds that they:
1. Locked in a deposit
2. Physically arrived at the location
3. Successfully completed the service

This creates a **self-correcting marketplace**. Customers gain bulletproof transparency into which businesses actually honor their slots, deliver premium service, and manage their escrows fairly. Businesses, in turn, earn an **un-gameable reputation score** that acts as their ultimate marketing asset. We have turned feedback from a subjective comment section into a certified, verifiable track record.

**How Business Scoring Works:**
- Only verified customers can review (deposit + GPS arrival confirmed)
- Reviews are weighted by Trust Score — higher-scoring users have more review weight
- Pattern detection identifies fake review rings automatically
- Business score decay: old reviews fade, recent experience matters most

## 9. Trust Score — How It Works, Plain English

### 9.1 What Is the Trust Score?

It is a number from **0 to 100** that predicts: *How likely is this person to show up for their next booking?*

It is:
- **Dynamic:** Changes with every booking
- **Transparent:** You see the number and the plain-English reasons
- **Non-punitive:** We tell you how to improve, never shame you
- **Portable:** It follows you to every Pabandi seller worldwide
- **Yours:** You can export it anytime

**What it is NOT:**
- Not a credit score from a bank
- Not permanent — old no-shows fade away
- Not a judgment of your character
- Not shared with sellers in raw form

### 9.2 What Shapes Your Score — The Ingredients

Think of it like making chai. The final taste depends on several ingredients:

| Ingredient | Simple explanation | How it affects your score |
|-----------|-------------------|--------------------------|
| **Show-up history** | How often you actually go vs. ghost | The biggest factor. Show up = go up |
| **Lead time** | How far ahead you book | Booking 3 days out = safer than booking in 20 minutes |
| **Day and time** | Monday 10 AM vs. Friday 10 PM | People keep Monday appointments better |
| **Service type** | Dentist vs. restaurant | Different services have different no-show rates |
| **Distance** | How far you travel | 2 km is easy; 20 km in traffic is harder |
| **Booking source** | Instagram DM, website, walk-in | Some channels have slightly different patterns |
| **Device check** | Is this the phone we recognize? | Helps stop fake accounts |
| **Pabandi Passport age** | How long you have used Pabandi | Optional bonus for loyal users |

**What we ignore:** Name, income, caste, address, contacts, messages, photos, location outside bookings.

### 9.3 Four AI Models, One Fair Decision

Instead of one mysterious algorithm, we use **four specialist models** that vote on your score. This is like asking four different experts and taking their average — fairer and more accurate.

**Expert 1: The Pattern Detective (Gradient Boosted Trees)**
- Looks at your booking history: attendance rate, lead time, day, venue type
- Finds patterns like: *"People who book 3+ days ahead and show up 90% of the time rarely ghost"*
- Fully explainable — shows you exactly what is helping or hurting your score

**Expert 2: The Memory Keeper (Temporal Graph Neural Network)**
- Sees your history as a web: you → places → time
- Recognizes loyalty: *"This person has been to Salon X every 6 weeks for 2 years — they are reliable"*
- Gives new users a fair chance by leaning on venue-level averages

**Expert 3: The Subtle Cue Catcher (Wide & Deep Neural Network)**
- Catches rare combinations: *"New device + 30-minute lead time + restaurant booking = slightly higher risk"*
- Catches patterns that are too subtle for traditional models
- Regularly audited to ensure no unfair bias creeps in

**Expert 4: The Fair Arbiter (Calibrated Meta-Learner)**
- Takes the other three models' opinions and makes the final call
- **Perfectly calibrated:** If it says 30% risk, that means exactly 30% — no more, no less
- This is how we guarantee deposits are *fair* — never a rupee more than your actual risk

If you feel a deposit was unfair:

2. A real human reviews it within **24 hours**

## 10. API for Developers — Complete Guide

The Pabandi API gives you access to the same smart fairness engine that powers our app. You can add it to any booking platform — salon software, restaurant reservation systems, clinic management tools, hotel booking engines, even live-selling and rental platforms.

**In plain terms:** You can make your app as smart as Pabandi with one phone call.

### 10.2 What You Get From One API Call

Send us a few details about a booking, and we return:

| Return value | What it means |
|-------------|---------------|
| `no_show_probability` | A number from 0.00 to 1.00 — the chance the person will not show |
| `recommended_deposit_percent` | The fair deposit percentage, 0–50 |
| `reason_codes` | Plain-English explanations like "short_lead_time" or "new_venue" |

| `model_version` | Which version of our AI made this prediction |
| `business_score_context` | Optional: how the business's reliability score affects the recommendation |

**What you never receive:**
- The buyer's exact Trust Score number
- The buyer's name, phone, email, or identity
- Other sellers' bookings or data
- Our model's secret weights

## 12. Halal Staking — Mudarabah Profit-Sharing

Pabandi's staking model is architecturally designed to comply with Islamic finance principles. All yield is sourced from genuine commercial activity — no interest, no speculation, no prohibited elements.

### No Ribā (Interest)

**Zero Fixed Returns**
Stakers receive no predetermined interest rate. All rewards are a proportional share of actual platform revenue (Mudarabah). If the platform earns more, stakers earn more — profit only, never guaranteed income.

### No Gharar (Uncertainty)

**Transparent Profit Pool**
The reward pool is funded on-chain from verified escrow commission and API fees. The profit-sharing ratio is fixed per epoch and publicly visible on-chain — no hidden terms or ambiguity.

### No Maysir (Gambling)

**Real Commercial Revenue**
Rewards are tied exclusively to booking platform fees — no randomness, no speculative instruments, no zero-sum mechanisms. Every PAB you earn traces to a real completed transaction.

### How the Mudarabah Profit-Share Works

**01 Lock $PAB Tokens**
Stake your PAB tokens for a minimum 30-day epoch. Your principal is locked in the smart contract — capital is always preserved.

**02 Platform Earns Revenue**
Pabandi collects 0.5% escrow commission + API fees from real bookings. This revenue is deposited on-chain into the epoch reward pool.

**03 Proportional Profit Distributed**
At epoch end, each staker receives: `Reward = Pool × (Your Stake / Total Staked)`. Pure proportional sharing — no favouritism, no fixed rate.

**04 Unstake Anytime After Lockup**
Once the lock period expires, withdraw your full principal. No deductions on capital — consistent with Islamic capital preservation principles.

### AAOIFI Compliant

Structured on AAOIFI Standard No. 13 (Mudarabah) and Standard No. 17 (Investment Sukuk). Profit-sharing ratio is epoch-fixed and publicly verifiable.

Smart contract: `PabandiHalalStaking.sol`

### Pabandi Halal Staking vs. Conventional DeFi

| Feature | Pabandi Halal Staking | Conventional DeFi Yield |
|---------|----------------------|------------------------|
| **Return Type** | Profit-share from real fees | Fixed APY (interest) |
| **Revenue Source** | Booking escrow + API fees | Lending, liquidity pools |
| **Guaranteed Return** | No — depends on revenue | Often yes — fixed rate |
| **Capital Protected** | Yes — principal returned in full | Impermanent loss possible |
| **Riba (Interest)** | ✗ None | ✓ Core mechanism |
| **Gharar** | ✗ None — on-chain transparency | ✓ Often present |
| **Sharia Status** | ✓ Halal | ✗ Prohibited |
