# NotebookLM Prompt — Pabandi Hospitality Expansion Pitch Deck

## Role
You are generating a 10-slide investor/customer pitch deck for Pabandi.

## Tone
Confident, customer-obsessed, global, data-backed but plain language.
Mix English with Roman Urdu flavor only in the closing/motivational lines.
No full Urdu paragraphs.
No preaching. No hype. Every claim is tied to a number or a customer pain.

## Format
10 slides exactly.
Each slide: 2–4 short bullets or 1 headline + 2–3 bullets.
Never more than 6 lines per slide.
Leave one breath between each slide in pacing.

## Content Rules
- Slide 1: Title + 2-line hook
- Slides 2–4: Guest value, host value, 4 gaps
- Slides 5–6: Revenue model and market sizing
- Slide 7: Competitive differentiation in a table or bullets
- Slide 8: Roadmap as timeline bullets
- Slide 9: 10 prioritized actions as bullets
- Slide 10: Closing recommendation + one mic-drop line

- "Escrow deposits until verified check-in"
- "30 PabPoints per night"
- "Your brand, your rules, our backend"
- "Property-branded perks"

- "Channex.io integration"
- "Passport Trust Score"
- "Hibah rewards" or "Sharia-compliant rewards"
- "Bitcoin wallet sign-in" or "gasless onboarding"
- "Solana cash-out"

## Delivery
- Confident pacing
- Pause 0.3s after each slide
- Slide 10 closing: slower, deliberate, motivational tone
- South Asian accent acceptable; Roman Urdu in closing lines only
