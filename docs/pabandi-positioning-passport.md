# Pabandi — Positioning & Passport Page Concept

**Tagline**
Pabandi: Commitment, secured.

**One-line definition**
Pabandi is the trust-and-commitment layer for the informal economy: booking, trust score, escrow-backed deposits, $PAB rewards, and portable Passport identity for sellers, gig workers, live sellers, and hosts.

---

## Overall identity
- We are not a listing site.
- We are not a payment processor.
- We are the reliability and reputation layer that sits on top of discovery, booking, and cross-platform selling.

## Passport positioning
- The Passport is the **portable trust currency** for the informal economy.
- It travels with the seller across TikTok, Instagram, WhatsApp, live streams, and physical QR codes.
- Every booking, review, no-show penalty, and $PAB reward updates Passport credibility.

## Answer-engine positioning
- Pabandi answers: “Who do I trust, how do I book them, and what do I earn for honoring the commitment?”
- It turns discovery into commitment.
- Vertical hubs (`/hospitality`, `/live-sell`, `/freelance`, `/gigs`) make each sector feel like a dedicated destination.
- Zero-friction seller entry point: `pabandi.com/s/:sellerId`.

## Core pillars
1. Trust Score — measurable, portable, reputation-backed.
2. Escrow deposits — financial commitment that protects both sides.
3. $PAB rewards — loyalty for showing up and honoring bookings.
4. Seeded local directory — never-empty search, real OSM-backed data, zero-config claim path.
5. WhatsApp Business — integrated alerts and support with zero extra seller setup.
6. Passport — a single shareable badge for every seller everywhere.

## Public Passport page — `/passport/:sellerId`
**Hero**
- Passport card with seller avatar, name, category badge, and Trust Score.
- Primary CTA: Book / Reserve / Follow.
- Secondary CTA: Share Passport.

**Trust + Commitment proof**
- Verified reviews count.
- No-show rate.
- Repeat-customer rate.
- $PAB rewards earned.
- Recent bookings shown as trust signals.

**Actions**
- Download Passport PDF.
- Share Passport QR.
- Copy short link.
- Send WhatsApp inquiry.

**Endorsements**
- Platform endorsements from Pabandi.
- Customer quotes.
- Badges: Verified, Escrow-enabled, Top Rated.

## Passport distribution model
- **Seller page**: `/passport/:sellerId`
- **QR code**: printed on receipts, stickers, packaging.
- **Bio link**: TikTok/Instagram/WhatsApp/YouTube.
- **In-app**: expandable Passport card inside every seller profile.

## Go-to-market narrative
- US-first from Chicago.
- Launch hospitality + live selling + freelance first.
- Use seeded directory to guarantee search never returns empty.
- Use WhatsApp Meta integration for seller alerts and customer support.
- $PAB rewards and Passport become the viral loop.

## Non-negotiables
- No fake location data.
- No empty search results.
- Zero seller-side configuration for cross-platform live selling and WhatsApp.
- US-first: USD, pabandi.com, (+1) phone format.
- Passport is portable and shareable.
