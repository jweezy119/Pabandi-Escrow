## Solution & Unique Value Proposition
**200-word limit**

The informal service economy still runs on trust and cash. Bookings get missed, payments get disputed, and reputation is fragmented across platforms. Pabandi fixes this with a portable trust layer: a publicly auditable Trust Score, Web3 escrow that funds the job—not the persona—and $PAB rewards that turn reliability into real value. For customers, it means booking verified local professionals with payment held safely until the job is done. For providers, it means fewer cancellations, faster visibility growth, and earnable tokens for showing up. We are building a system where one good job compounds into more opportunity without cold outreach. That is why users don’t just want Pabandi; they need it.
